Project Summary - The Rise of CSGO

Topic and Purpose

The purpose of the 'The Rise of CSGO' website is to provide fans of Counter-Strike: Global Offensive with a dedicated space to explore and relive the most iconic moments in the game's history. It serves as a repository for highlights from top players, best-performing teams, and memorable in-game events.

Extras Used

  • Smooth Scrolling Navigation: Enhances the user experience by providing a smooth and visually pleasing transition between different sections of the website.
  • Video Embedding: Utilizes iframes to embed video content directly on the website, allowing users to watch CSGO highlights without needing to navigate away from the page.

Plugin Documentation

No plugins were used in the creation of this website.
