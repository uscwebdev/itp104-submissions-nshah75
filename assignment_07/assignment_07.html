Final Project Proposal

Topic:

This website will be about the game Counter Strike: Global Offensive. It will include the history of CSGO, some of the best teams, some of the best players to ever do it, and some cool highlights to look at.

Audience:

My intended audience would be passionate gamers in general, but more specifically those that are fans of the game.

Design & Style:

It will have the simple and soft background colors, with bright font colors to make the information more digestable. The color of the text would be a lighter version of the background color, while numbers would have more of a contrasting color scheme.

Primary Color: #1B2A41 (Background Color)

Secondary Color: #A9FFF7 (Text color)

Secondary Color: #FCCA46 (Numbers color)

Tertiary Color: #EEE5E9 (Heading color)

Links to Inspiration Websites:

  • HLTV (Official CSGO website)
  • ESL (Famous CSGO event)
  • Liquipedia (Official CSGO statistics)

Scope:

There will be 4 pages in my website. 1 page for the introduction and history of CSGO, 1 page about the best players, 1 page about the best teams, and 1 page with some iconic highlights.
